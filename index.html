About Me:

My name is Cristian Saez (Ph.D. in Astronomy & Astrophysics at Penn State University) and I am currently a Postdoc at Universidad de Chile.

My research interests gravitate mainly around three extra-galactic topics which are: quasar winds, galactic evolution in protoclusters, and the study of the cluster dark haloes.

My collaborations have around nine-hundredths citations from which around three-hundredths come from my first and second author papers; my h-index is 18. I have six years of Postdoctoral work and around four years of teaching experience (two years full-time).
