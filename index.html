My name is Cristian Saez, holding a Ph.D. in Astronomy & Astrophysics from Penn State University. Formerly a researcher, I am now focusing on advancing my career in Computer Science and pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science. My current interests lie in software development, data science, and computational modeling.

My background in astronomy includes research on quasar winds, galactic evolution in protoclusters, and dark halo studies. I have contributed to over 1000 citations, with an h-index of 20, and accumulated extensive experience in teaching and collaborative projects.
